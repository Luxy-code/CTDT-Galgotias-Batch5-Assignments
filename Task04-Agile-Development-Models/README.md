## 🎧 Agile Board Breakdown: Smart Wireless Headset

To visually represent and manage tasks, workflows, and progress throughout the product lifecycle, we have created a **Kanban Agile Board**. The board tracks cross-functional tickets across 5 key phases of the sprint.

### 🏷️ Epic / Categories
The tasks are color-coded into three main functional epics:
- 🔵 **Development:** Firmware, audio processing algorithms, noise reduction.
- 🟢 **Design (Hardware):** PCB layout, signal integrity, power management, ergonomics.
- 🟠 **Marketing:** Product launch strategy, press releases, promo videos.

### 📊 Sprint Workflow Columns

#### 1️⃣ Product Backlog
- `[DEV-04]` OTA Firmware Update System
- `[MKT-03]` Online Retailer Distribution Agreements
- `[DES-04]` Packaging Box Design

#### 2️⃣ To Do (Current Sprint)
- `[DEV-03]` Voice Activity Detection Algorithm
- `[MKT-02]` Promo Video Production

#### 3️⃣ In Progress
- `[DES-02]` PCB Layout & EMI Mitigation
- `[DEV-02]` Active Noise Reduction (ANC) Audio Processing

#### 4️⃣ Review & Testing
- `[DES-01]` Ergonomic Ear-cup Molding Prototype
- `[DEV-01]` Bluetooth 5.3 Pairing Protocols

#### 5️⃣ Done
- `[MKT-01]` Press Release Draft for Launch Event
- `[DES-00]` Power Management Circuitry (Battery Life)
