## 📖 Storyboard Breakdown: NutriPrep Smart Meal App

To ensure cohesive design thinking, we are using the **NutriPrep Smart Meal App** (designed for our "Busy Brian" persona from Task 04). The storyboard illustrates the exact pain points and how the product resolves them in 6 distinct scenes.

### 🎬 Scene 1: The Exhaustion
- **Visual:** Brian sitting at his desk late at night, rubbing his eyes.
- **Caption:** It's 8:30 PM. Brian just finished a 10-hour coding sprint. He is completely drained and starving.

### 🎬 Scene 2: The Frustration
- **Visual:** Brian opens his fridge. It contains only rotting vegetables.
- **Dialogue:** "Ugh, I don't have the energy to chop broccoli. Looks like it's greasy takeout again..."

### 🎬 Scene 3: The Discovery
- **Visual:** Brian scrolling on his phone; an ad for NutriPrep pops up.
- **Caption:** While waiting for his junk food, he sees an ad for NutriPrep: "Healthy meals on autopilot."

### 🎬 Scene 4: The Action
- **Visual:** Screen recording of the app; Brian selects 'High Protein' and his delivery schedule.
- **Caption:** He downloads it, connects his fitness tracker, and sets up a weekly subscription in just 3 clicks.

### 🎬 Scene 5: The Solution
- **Visual:** A delivery rider handing Brian a sleek, insulated meal box exactly at 1:00 PM the next day.
- **Dialogue:** "Wow, that arrived right on schedule without me having to order it today!"

### 🎬 Scene 6: The Resolution
- **Visual:** Brian eating a healthy meal while coding, looking energized and happy.
- **Caption:** He eats healthy, saves time, hits his gym goals, and has zero food waste. Problem solved.
